Core Services

Talent acquisition:

KR Staffing helps companies identify, attract, and hire the best candidates for their open positions. This includes sourcing candidates through various channels, such as job boards, social media, and professional networks.

Resume review and candidate evaluation:

KR Staffing's team of experienced recruiters can review and evaluate resumes to ensure that they meet the criteria set by the hiring company. They can also help candidates prepare for interviews and negotiate job offers.

Onboarding support:

Once a candidate has been hired, KR Staffing can provide support with onboarding to ensure a smooth transition into the new role. This can include paperwork, orientation, and introduction to the company culture.

Temporary staffing:

KR Staffing can also provide temporary staffing solutions for companies that need short-term or project-based help. This can be a cost-effective way for companies to meet their staffing needs without committing to a full-time hire.

Overall, KR Staffing aims to provide a full range of recruitment solutions to help companies find and hire the best talent for their open positions.
